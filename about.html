My Story

Why I Built KCSE Vault

I've been a secondary school teacher for over a decade. Every year, I watch students who work hard, attend every class, and genuinely want to succeed — but still struggle in the exam room because they don't have the right revision materials.

"Good notes shouldn't be a privilege. Every student in Kenya deserves access to quality revision materials — whether they're in Nairobi or a rural school in Turkana."

After years of sharing handwritten notes with my own students, I decided to package and share them with Kenya — and that's how KCSE Vault was born.

Every resource on this site has been written, refined and tested in a real classroom. These aren't copied from textbooks — they're built from years of teaching experience and understanding exactly where students go wrong.
