Complete Guide to CBC Resources in Kenya

📅 Updated: June 2026 • ⏱ 7 min read

The Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC) is Kenya's new education system designed to develop learners' skills and competencies. With the transition from 8-4-4 to CBC, finding quality resources has become essential for teachers, students, and parents.

📌 Key Fact: CBC focuses on what learners can do, not just what they know. Resources should support skill development and practical application.

1. What is CBC?

The Competency-Based Curriculum is Kenya's education reform that emphasizes:

  • Practical Skills - Applying knowledge in real situations
  • Critical Thinking - Analyzing and solving problems
  • Creativity - Innovative thinking and expression
  • Collaboration - Working with others effectively
  • Values Integration - Character development and ethics

8. Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between CBC and 8-4-4?

CBC focuses on competencies and skills, while 8-4-4 emphasizes content knowledge and examinations. CBC uses continuous assessment while 8-4-4 relies primarily on final exams.
